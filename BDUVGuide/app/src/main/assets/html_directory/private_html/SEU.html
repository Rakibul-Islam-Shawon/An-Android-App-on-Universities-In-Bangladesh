Southeast University
Introduction of University
Southeast University (Bengali: ???? ???? ??????????????) or SEU is a private university in Banani, Dhaka, Bangladesh. It is one of the reputed universities in Bangladesh. The University was established under the Private University Act, 1992 approved by the Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh. Its permanent campus is in 251/A & 252, Tejgaon Industrial Area, Dhaka-1208, Bangladesh. SEU ranked 11th among private universities in Bangla Tribune University Ranking 2017 (Perceptual Ranking).

Mission

The mission of SEU is to provide higher education to students of Bangladesh at an affordable cost, keeping in view the challenges of the twenty first century. And this mission will be achieved by imparting world class education and providing research facilities so that the students can attain their full intellectual, social and personal potentials. SEU is committed to develop human capital by cultivating creative thinking in individuals.

Vision

The vision of Southeast University (SEU) is to flourish as a prestigious seat of learning in the South Asian region. As a center of excellence, it aims at attracting students from all countries of the region.

Goal & Scope

The goal of the University is to provide excellent modern education for students with a view to equipping them with the skill and knowledge necessary to lead the country in its quest for development. Along with this, the University also aims at producing students of high moral standard in its students. Southeast University (SEU) has the authority, under its charter, to provide instruction to confer Undergraduate and Post Graduate degrees. It has plans to offer MPhil. and PhD programs in all branches of higher studies including engineering, agriculture, language, law and medical sciences. It has also the authority to grant diplomas, certificates and other academic distinctions. It has also the authority to open new Schools of studies and new institutions on its own campus. The University is run by a number of statutory bodies as required under the provisions of the Private University Act, 1992.

Centra Library
Prologue

The academic libraries have been described as the heart of the learning community, providing a place for students and faculty to conduct their research and advance their knowledge. Without library an educational institution is a body without soul. Keeping this view in mind, Southeast University Library came into existence with inception of the university itself. Now the SEU library is a web based modern library which has a rich collection of both physical and online resources. For pursuing study and research library facilities are open to all students, teachers, Officers, staff and patrons associated with this university. SEU library acts as a centre point supporting academic and research activities of students, researchers, faculty members and Southeast University community. The Library is equipped with valuable books, journals, periodicals, research reports, government publications and audio visual materials. Professional and experienced staff members provide services and ensure facilities to SEU community. The SEU library is an innovative centre for academic excellence that advances the teaching, learning and research to diverse community by providing outstanding services and access to high quality printed and electronic resources.

Mission & Vision

Provide quality library and information services to empower the University reaching aim of delivering education facilities to learners, learning, teaching and research facilities to SEU community.

The main objective of the University library is to collect and make information available to contribute to quality of research, teaching and dissemination of knowledge.

To organize the conventional and non conventional information sources in a systematic manner to provide efficient and prompt service to users.

To disseminate information through variety of services for the benefit of SEU community to keep them updated/abreast with the latest developments in their areas of interest. To provide faster and efficient information services using information and communication technologies.

To provide user services with professional excellence focusing to SEU goals and objectives

Admission
Course Waiver

Course waiver allowed for Diploma Engineers for admission in B.Sc. Programs is subject to the condition that candidates have minimum CGPA of 3.00 or 1st class in waved course(s) from previously attended institutions. No waiver will be given on major course(s) and internship/ project work. Individual department may have some additional requirements.

Admission on Credit Transfer

Students desiring to transfer their credits are expected to have at least a CGPA of 2.5 (Grade B) in all previous exams to be considered for admission to the Southeast University. Up to 50% of the total credit hours in the undergraduate program will be accepted. Students intending to transfer to SEU must provide supporting documents like official transcript, course out line, etc, for the course(s) to be considered for transfer. Individual department may have some additional requirements.

Provisional Admission

SEU may conditionally accept applicants for admission who cannot produce official transcripts, certificates and other necessary documents at the time of admission. As a prerequisite all provisionally admitted students will be required to submit the appropriate credentials to continue. Required credentials must be submitted within the semester of initial enrollment.

Admission Procedure:
